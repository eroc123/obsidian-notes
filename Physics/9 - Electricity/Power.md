- Power is the rate of work being done (P=W/t)
	- Potential difference is the work done per unit charge (V = W/Q)
	- Current is the rate of flow of charge (I = Q/t)
	- **Derive:**
		- $P = QV/t$
		- It=Q
		- P=ItV/t
		- P=IV
- V = IR can also be used to express P=IV in terms of R
