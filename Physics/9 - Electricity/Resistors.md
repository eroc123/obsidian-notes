
## Potential Difference Rule 
### In series


$$V = V_1 + V_2 + V_3$$
$$V_1 = IR_1$$
$$V_2 = IR_2$$
$$V_3 = IR_3$$

$$R = {V\over I} = {IR_1 + IR_2 + IR_3 \over I}$$
### In parallel
$$I = I_1+I_2$$
$$V = V_1 = V_2$$
$$\text{Using Ohm's Law : }I = V/R$$
$$I_1 = {V \over R_1} + {V \over R_2}$$
Divide by V throughout:
$${1\over R} = {1 \over R_1} + {1 \over R_2}$$
